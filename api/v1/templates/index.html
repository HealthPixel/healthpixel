WHAT WE DO (ABOUT US)

We believe the patient registration process at hospitals can be significantly shortened. That's why we created HealthPixel—to streamline the way healthcare professionals access and update patient medical records. The platform allows for quick retrieval of a patient’s medical history and real-time updates using their email, all while ensuring data remains secure and accessible. It also provides features for registering new patients and allowing them to log in to view prescribed medications. With HealthPixel, the hassle of repeatedly recounting your medical history or filling out multiple forms at each new hospital is greatly reduced, thus getting the patient access to the medical care they need as quickly as possible.
